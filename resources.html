Resources

Trusted sources for deepening your understanding

To deepen your understanding of the Catholic faith, we recommend consulting primary sources and trusted explanations. Below are links to foundational documents and helpful websites.

  • The Holy Bible

    The inspired Word of God. Recommended translations include the RSV-2CE (Revised Standard Version - Second Catholic Edition) and the NABRE (New American Bible Revised Edition).

    Read the Bible Online (USCCB)

  • Catechism of the Catholic Church

    A systematic presentation of the faith, including the Creed, the Sacraments, the Commandments, and Prayer. It is the sure norm for teaching the faith.

    Read the Catechism

  • Vatican Website

    The official website of the Holy See, containing encyclicals, homilies, and news from the Pope and the Roman Curia.

    Visit Vatican.va

  • Catholic Answers

    A lay apostolate dedicated to explaining and defending the Catholic faith. A great resource for specific questions.

    Visit Catholic Answers

  • New Advent

    Features the Catholic Encyclopedia, writings of the Church Fathers, and the Summa Theologica of St. Thomas Aquinas.

    Visit New Advent
